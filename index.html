This website contains links to 14 single serving sites, connected as a webring. The websites take their starting points in Wikipedia articles linking to the Current Events page. The SSS:s were made in January 2026 by the first year students in the Interactive design course at the Visual Communication program at Beckmans Collage of Design in Stockholm, Sweden. The course is supervised by senior lecturer Peter Ström.
